How it works
  1. Enter 4 players and choose umpire rotation (auto/manual, each round - one different umpire).
  2. Four rounds: 1) Hollywood (Movies/Web Series), 2) Indian (Movies/Web Series), 3) Dialogues, 4) Eyes.
  3. 15sec timer per question. First correct among the three non‑umpire players gets +10.
  4. If no player gets it, the umpire may answer. If correct, umpire gets +15.
  5. Click Content packs to paste/upload your own JSON with image URLs & answers. Umpire can add questions on the fly in the round screen if needed.

Note: Don't refresh the browser. Refreshing will restart the game. Nothing is uploaded; all runs locally in your browser.
